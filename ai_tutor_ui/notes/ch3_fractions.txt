Chapter 3: Fractions

Fractions are a way of representing parts of a whole. A fraction has two numbers: 
the numerator (top) and the denominator (bottom).

Examples:
1/2 means one part out of two equal parts.
3/4 means three parts out of four equal parts.

Key Points:
- Equivalent fractions: 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8
- Simplifying fractions: 6/8 = 3/4
- Adding fractions: 1/4 + 2/4 = 3/4
- Mixed numbers: 1 1/2 = 3/2
